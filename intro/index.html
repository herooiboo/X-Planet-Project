Introduction

        Mars is one of the most mysterious secrets in our solar system so NASA took advantage of this and undertook many missions to visit or orbit this planet. Astronauts always seek to explore it by sending missions to learn about him from all over the world. Humans have launched many missions, including successful and unsuccessful, since 1960. Where the United States launched 16 successful missions and 6 unsuccessful missions. Russian launched 3 successful missions and 15 unsuccessful missions. Indian has launched a once successful mission to Mars and the space agency launched once a successful mission.

Our mission to present data about mars, it’s missions, and the challenges that face astronauts on it in the form of mobile application, web application, and a short film about mars. We will divide the information in the application into chapters for example the first chapter will talk about mars and its physical properties, size, gravity, etc. Second, the missions that failed to reach Mars and the causes of failure. NASA systems to communicate, or NASA's eyes for example Deep Space Network (DSN) and Deep Space Optical Communications (DSOC).

In addition to work to find an idea to improve these systems maybe we will do a call from mars to earth or to connect directly without using a mars orbit telescope so our solution to find an idea to develop a Deep Space Network (DSN) to connect with astronauts directly that will save time instead of having the call arrive after half an hour, it may arrive after a quarter of an hour.

Finally, it is great to find an app that contains all the information about Mars, and this is what we will do to facilitate the search by collecting all this data in one place only. Astronaut, pay attention, we will hear you from the earth!
